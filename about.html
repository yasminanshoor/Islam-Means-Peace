About





This Campaign Aims To Tackle Prejudice Against Muslims


Many misconceptions of Islam and Muslims have led many people to develop stigmas and stereotypes about the religion as a whole. Islam is viewed as a violent religion to some, who believe that the violent acts of minority terrorist organisations represent the peaceful religion of Islam. It is important for these people to understand that these terrorist organisations do not represent Islam at all, and for them to understand what Islam truly stands for. It is not a religion of violence, but rather one that teaches peace and humility


Our Goals

Spread awareness of this prejudice
Remove negative stereotypes that result in Islamaphobia
Take a step closer to a better, less prejudice world
